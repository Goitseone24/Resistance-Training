Calisthenics Plans

Bodyweight exercises for heart health

Beginner Calisthenics Routine

Week 1-4 Foundation

  • Wall Pushups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Assisted Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Seated Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Perform 3 times per week with rest days in between

Supporting Research

This program is based on findings from:

Journal of Hypertension: Calisthenics for Blood Pressure Control

Intermediate Progressions

Week 5-8 Progression

  • Incline Pushups: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Plank to Pushup: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Australian Pullups: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
