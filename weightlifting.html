Weightlifting Plans

Resistance training for cardiovascular health

Beginner Weightlifting Routine

Week 1-4 Foundation

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Goblet Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Seated Row Machine: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 2 sets of 10-12 reps

Perform 2-3 times per week with rest days in between

Supporting Research

This program is based on findings from:

NIH Study: Resistance Training and Cardiovascular Health

Intermediate Progressions

Week 5-8 Progression

  • Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Barbell Squats: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
