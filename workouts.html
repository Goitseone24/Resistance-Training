Bodyweight Mastery Programs

Beginner Foundation

Beginner

Build fundamental strength and control with these essential bodyweight movements. Perfect for those new to calisthenics.

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Inverted Rows: 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
  • Assisted Pull-ups: 3 sets of 3-5 reps

Frequency: 3 times per week

Duration: 6-8 weeks

Equipment: Pull-up bar, resistance bands

Skill Development

Intermediate

Develop advanced calisthenics skills and strength to take your training to the next level.

  • Archer Push-ups: 3 sets of 5-8 reps per side
  • Pistol Squats: 3 sets of 5 reps per leg
  • Pull-ups: 4 sets of 6-10 reps
  • L-sit Hold: 3 sets of 15-30 seconds
  • Handstand Practice: 5 minutes daily

Frequency: 4 times per week

Duration: 8-12 weeks

Equipment: Parallel bars, yoga mat

Advanced Strength

Advanced

Master advanced moves like muscle-ups and planche progressions with this challenging routine.

  • Muscle-ups: 5 sets of 3-5 reps
  • Front Lever Progressions: 4 sets of 8-12s holds
  • Planche Lean: 4 sets of 15-20s
  • Handstand Push-ups: 4 sets of 5-8 reps
  • Weighted Pull-ups: 4 sets of 4-6 reps

Frequency: 5 times per week

Duration: 12+ weeks

Equipment: Rings, weight vest

Strength Training Programs

Basic Strength

Barbell Dumbbells

Build foundational strength with these essential compound lifts. Perfect for beginners.

  • Barbell Squat: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Deadlift: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 6 reps
  • Barbell Row: 3 sets of 8 reps

Frequency: 3-4 times per week

Duration: 8-12 weeks

Equipment: Full gym recommended

Muscle Building

Full Gym

Focus on muscle growth with this high-volume bodybuilding-style program.

  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lat Pulldown: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12 reps

Frequency: 5 days per week

Duration: 6-8 weeks

Equipment: Full gym required

Power & Performance

Olympic Plyometric

Develop explosive power with Olympic lifts and plyometric exercises.

  • Power Cleans: 5 sets of 3 reps
  • Push Press: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Box Jumps: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Front Squat: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Medicine Ball Throws: 3 sets of 8 reps

Frequency: 4 times per week

Duration: 6 weeks

Equipment: Olympic barbell, boxes
